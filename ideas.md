**Design Movement:** Bauhaus-meets-Editorial Fashion
**Core Principles:**
1. Strong typographic hierarchy using serif display + sans-serif body
2. High-contrast black/cream palette with a single vivid accent (rose/coral)
3. Structured grid with intentional asymmetry for visual tension
4. Minimal decoration — let content and whitespace breathe

**Color Philosophy:** Cream (#FAF7F2) background with near-black (#1A1A1A) text. A warm rose-coral (#E8637A) accent for interactive elements and highlights. This evokes high-fashion editorial magazines — authoritative yet approachable.

**Layout Paradigm:** Left-anchored header with a vertical tab rail on the left side (desktop) collapsing to a horizontal scrollable strip on mobile. Content area occupies 70% of the viewport width, creating an editorial "column" feel.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thick left-border accent lines on section headings
- Oversized tab numbers (01–07) in light gray behind tab titles
- Thin horizontal rules between sections

**Interaction Philosophy:** Tabs slide content in from the right; hover states use underline-grow rather than background fills.

**Animation:** Subtle fade+slide (200ms ease-out) on tab change. Progress bar fills smoothly with cubic-bezier.

**Typography System:** Display — Playfair Display (serif, bold). Body — DM Sans (400/500). Tab labels — DM Sans 600 uppercase tracked.

**Signature Elements:**
- Hard-shadow boxes (4px offset, no blur)
- Uppercase condensed headings
- Diagonal stripe patterns as decorative dividers

**Interaction Philosophy:** Buttons invert on hover (black bg → white text). Active tab gets a thick underline border.

**Animation:** Instant tab switches with a brief flash effect. No easing — snappy and direct.

**Typography System:** Display — Space Grotesk (700). Body — Space Grotesk (400). Numbers — Space Mono.

- Soft parchment card backgrounds with subtle inner shadows

## Selected Design: Soft Atelier / Couture Studio

Warm parchment tones, elegant Cormorant Garamond display type, dusty rose accents, and a couture-studio aesthetic. This perfectly matches the Fashion PBL theme and supports bilingual Hebrew/English with graceful RTL mirroring.
